✦ Suzy's Golden Coffee Recipe
a smooth, chocolatey South American cup — perfected over many mornings ☕
// what you need

Start with 18g of freshly ground South American beans — medium-fine grind. Heat your water to exactly 82°C. Any hotter and you'll scorch the delicate chocolate notes. Your target is 270ml total at a 1:15 ratio.

// the bloom

Pour just enough water to saturate the grounds — about 35–40ml — then wait. Watch the coffee swell up into a beautiful mushroom shape 🍄. This is the bloom, and it takes 35 seconds. Don't rush it. The CO₂ escaping means your beans are fresh and the extraction will be even.

// three-stage pour

After the bloom, pour in three gentle, circular stages:

01
40ml bloom pour — slow and centered
02
110ml second pour — spiral outward gently
03
120ml final pour — steady and patient
// the finish

Everything should drain by 2:30. If it's faster, grind finer next time. Slower? Go coarser. When it's right, you'll get a cup that's smooth, round, and gently chocolatey 🍫 — no bitterness, no sourness. Just a really good morning.
